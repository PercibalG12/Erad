What is ERAD?

ERAD

ERAD was formed by a group of Kingston College students, to name a few Malikai Allwood, Percival Roberts and Michael Coke with the intention of aiding studenst not only in Jamaica but the Caribbean in their upcoming CXC exams. Since then our efforts have been recoginized by Smile Jamaica.
